[PAGE SUMMARY]
This is Rutgers University New Brunswick's official off-campus housing marketplace. It is intended for students looking for apartments, houses, sublets, and roommate options near Rutgers New Brunswick. The page supports filtering by beds, baths, maximum rent, price per unit or per bed, style, amenities, availability, and commute time.

The marketplace frames itself as a centralized place to contact landlords near Rutgers New Brunswick instead of browsing many separate rental websites. Listings can include rent, availability, bed/bath count, amenities, address, contact information, and estimated walking/biking/transit commute distance from campus points.

[RUTGERS MARKETPLACE NOTES]
- Listings are searchable by apartment or house type.
- Students can compare prices, unit features, and availability.
- Commute information is useful because Rutgers New Brunswick is spread across several areas and bus routes.
- The page includes landlord-facing functionality, but students are not required to use any particular landlord.
- The Rutgers Off-Campus Living office provides a listing location, but students should still inspect rentals, read leases carefully, and evaluate landlords independently.
